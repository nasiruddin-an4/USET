Student Clubs & Organizations

USET offers a wide range of student clubs and organizations that cater to various interests and passions. These student-led groups provide opportunities to pursue hobbies, develop leadership skills, and connect with like-minded peers.

Benefits of Joining Clubs:

  • Develop leadership and teamwork skills
  • Apply classroom learning to real-world projects
  • Build a network of friends and professional contacts
  • Enhance your resume with extracurricular achievements

Tech Innovators Club

A community of tech enthusiasts who work on innovative projects, participate in hackathons, and organize workshops on emerging technologies.

Housing & Dining Services

USET is primarily a commuter campus but offers partnerships with nearby residential facilities for students seeking housing options. Our campus dining facilities provide convenient and affordable meal options for the entire university community.

Housing Options

Partnered accommodations provide safe and convenient living options near campus.

Partner Accommodations:
  • Samad-Banu Student Housing (5 min walk)
  • Narayanganj Student Residences (15 min by transport)
Housing Assistance:
  • Housing referral services
  • Roommate matching assistance
  • Housing fairs

Dining Services

Variety of dining options to meet diverse tastes and dietary needs.

Campus Dining Locations:
  • Main Cafeteria (Student Center)
  • Green Café (Library)
  • Engineering Building Kiosk
Meal Plan Options:
  • Prepaid meal cards
  • Monthly meal plans
  • Dietary accommodations
