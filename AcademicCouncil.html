Meeting Highlights

USET successfully hosted its inaugural Academic Council Meeting at the University’s Conference Room.

The meeting, chaired by Prof. Dr. Engr. A.K.M. Fazlul Haque, brought together distinguished academics from the University of Dhaka and senior USET officials. The session focused on strengthening academic governance, research excellence, and global academic standards.

Key Agenda
  • Approval of academic policies
  • Curriculum development and modernization
  • Faculty recruitment and quality assurance
  • Alignment with global academic benchmarks
In line with the Private University Act 2010 and UGC Bangladesh guidelines.

“Today’s meeting is a historic beginning for USET. We are committed to building a skill-driven, research-oriented, and globally connected university.”

Prof. Dr. Engr. A.K.M. Fazlul Haque
Vice-Chancellor (Designate), USET
