About USET

Bangladesh's first skill-based university dedicated to practical education and industry-relevant training.

Our Mission

To provide industry-focused, skill-based education that prepares students for immediate employability and professional success while contributing to the socio-economic development of Bangladesh.

USET is committed to delivering practical education and hands-on skills through practical training, industrial partnerships, and a focus on real-world application of academic concepts.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Bangladesh's leading institution for skill-based higher education, creating a new generation of workforce-ready graduates who contribute to national development through skill-based innovation.

We aspire to bridge the gap between academic education and professional requirements, revolutionizing higher education in Bangladesh through our focus on practical skills and industry relevance.

Conceptualization

2018

The idea for Bangladesh's first skill-based university was conceived by a group of educators and industry leaders concerned about the gap between traditional education and workplace requirements.

Planning & Approvals

2019

The founding team developed the university's curriculum framework based on international polytechnic models and secured approvals from the Bangladesh University Grants Commission.

Foundation & Launch

2020

USET was officially established with its first campus in Narayanganj, offering four initial programs designed with direct input from industry partners. The first batch of students was admitted in Fall 2020.

Growth & Recognition

Present

Today, USET continues to expand its program offerings, establish new industry partnerships, and gain recognition for its innovative approach to higher education in Bangladesh.

Modern Labs

State-of-the-art computer and engineering laboratories equipped with the latest technology and industry-standard tools.

Digital Library

Comprehensive digital library with access to international journals, e-books, and research resources.

Collaboration Spaces

Modern spaces designed for group work, industry collaboration, and innovative project development.
