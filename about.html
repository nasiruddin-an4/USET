About USET

Bangladesh's first skill-based university dedicated to practical education and industry-relevant training.

Our Mission

To provide industry-focused, skill-based education that prepares students for immediate employability and professional success while contributing to the socio-economic development of Bangladesh.

USET is committed to delivering practical education and hands-on skills through practical training, industrial partnerships, and a focus on real-world application of academic concepts.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Bangladesh's leading institution for skill-based higher education, creating a new generation of workforce-ready graduates who contribute to national development through skill-based innovation.

We aspire to bridge the gap between academic education and professional requirements, revolutionizing higher education in Bangladesh through our focus on practical skills and industry relevance.

2023

Foundation of USET

Establishment of Bangladesh's first skill-based university with a vision to transform higher education.

2024

First Academic Year

Launch of initial programs with industry-aligned curriculum and practical training focus.
