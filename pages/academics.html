Our Academic Vision

USET is committed to providing industry-relevant education that prepares students for the demands of today's workplace.

Our academic programs are designed with direct input from industry leaders to ensure that graduates possess both theoretical knowledge and practical skills needed for professional success.

We follow the dual technical education system modeled after successful European approaches, combining classroom learning with hands-on experience through laboratory work, projects, and internships.

Practice-Oriented Learning: Emphasis on practical skills development

Industry Integration: Curriculum designed with industry partners

Professional Mentorship: Faculty with extensive industry experience

Academic Systems & Standards

Our commitment to academic excellence and quality education

Grading System

USET follows a standard letter grading system to evaluate student performance:

Grade Points Percentage
A+ 4.00 90-100%
A 3.75 85-89%
A- 3.50 80-84%
B+ 3.25 75-79%
B 3.00 70-74%

Credit System

Our programs follow a credit hour system that measures academic workload:

  • 1 Credit Hour: 15 hours of classroom instruction per semester
  • Laboratory/Practical: 30-45 hours of supervised laboratory work equals 1 credit hour
  • Internship/Fieldwork: 45 hours of supervised practical training equals 1 credit hour
  • Undergraduate Degree: Typically requires 120-144 credit hours
  • Graduate Degree: Typically requires 30-36 credit hours

Academic Calendar

The academic year at USET is divided into two main semesters with an optional summer term:

  • Registration: Late August
  • Classes Begin: First week of September
  • Midterm Exams: Late October
  • Final Exams: Mid-December
  • Semester Break: Late December - Early January

  • Registration: Early January
  • Classes Begin: Mid-January
  • Midterm Exams: Early March
  • Final Exams: Early May
  • Semester Break: Mid-May - June

  • Registration: Late May
  • Classes Begin: First week of June
  • Final Exams: Early August
  • Break: Mid-August - Late August
