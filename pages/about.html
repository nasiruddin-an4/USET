Bangladesh's First
Skill-Based University

University of Skill Enrichment & Technology (USET) is pioneering a new approach to higher education in Bangladesh.

Established in 2020, USET was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. We focus on practical, skill-based education that prepares students for the real-world challenges of their chosen professions.

Our curriculum follows the dual technical education system modeled after successful European approaches, combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on training to ensure graduates are job-ready from day one.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our educational philosophy

Our Mission

To provide accessible, industry-relevant education that equips students with practical skills and knowledge needed for professional success in a rapidly changing global economy.

  • Deliver practical, skill-based education
  • Focus on employability and industry integration
  • Foster innovation and entrepreneurship
  • Serve disadvantaged communities with quality education

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier institution for skill-based education in Bangladesh, contributing to national development through innovation, industry partnership, and human capital development.

  • Become a center of excellence for practical education
  • Lead innovation in teaching methodologies
  • Create industry partnerships that enhance student outcomes
  • Support Bangladesh's journey to developed nation status

Our History

The journey of building Bangladesh's first skill-based university

2020: Foundation

USET was established as the first skill-based university in Bangladesh, with a mission to provide practical, industry-relevant education. The initial campus was set up in Narayanganj, with a focus on serving students from areas with limited access to quality higher education.

2021: First Academic Programs

USET launched its first set of academic programs in Computer Science & Engineering, Business Administration, English, and Economics. These programs were designed with input from industry leaders to ensure relevance to market needs.

2022: Industry Partnerships

We established key partnerships with leading companies in Bangladesh to provide internship opportunities, guest lectures, and collaborative research projects for our students. These partnerships have been instrumental in enhancing the practical aspects of our education.

2023: Growth & Recognition

USET celebrated its growing student body and received recognition for its innovative approach to education. The university expanded its facilities and introduced new specializations to meet emerging industry demands.
