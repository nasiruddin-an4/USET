A Message for Our Faculty

At USET, our faculty members are passionate about delivering a transformative educational experience that balances theoretical knowledge with practical skills. We bring our industry experience and academic expertise into the classroom to prepare students for real-world challenges.

Our teaching philosophy emphasizes hands-on learning, problem-solving, and developing the critical thinking abilities essential for professional success. We are committed to mentoring students not just as learners, but as future professionals and innovators.

We invite you to explore our departments and get to know the dedicated individuals who make USET's unique educational approach possible.

Why Teach at USET?

Innovative Teaching

At USET, faculty members have the opportunity to implement innovative teaching methods that combine theoretical instruction with practical applications, creating a dynamic learning environment.

Industry Links

Our faculty maintain strong connections with industry partners, allowing them to bring real-world problems and opportunities into the classroom while staying current with industry trends and practices.

Research Focus

Faculty at USET engage in applied research that addresses real-world problems, particularly those relevant to Bangladesh's development needs, with opportunities for both personal research and collaborative projects.
